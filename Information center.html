Powder coating VS Cerakoting

Both powder coating and cerakoting are popular coating options that offer superior performance and aesthetics compared to traditional paint. Explore the differences with the articles below.

Powder Coating


Excellent for corrosion and chemical resistance, good electrical insulation. Not ideal for high-heat applications.


Article

Cerakoting


Better terms of heat resistance, making it suitable for engine components. Exceptional UV resistance and flexibility.
